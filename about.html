Who am IGiorgio
Rando

Information Security Specialist. Passionate about Etical Hacking and related issues from a young age. My approach to the world of computer science began with the bachelor's degree in Computer Science at the University of Udine, where I had the opportunity to get in touch with the Computer Science's base of. Later, noting the increasingly presence of network threats risk, I decided to specialize in Information Security (or Cyber Security), achieving a Master's degree in Information Security at the University of Milan.

I had the luck and the pleasure of coming into contact with different cultures and realities, having the possibility of studying abroad several times: for a short period I lived in Dublin attending at the International School of English; subsequently due to the Erasmus) I had the pleasure of increasing my knowledge in Cyber Security by attending Uppsala Universitet in Sweden.

After completing the education, it was possible for me to "exploit" my knowledge acquired in the Security field at the service of companies, such as Security Architect and Security Engineer. During the "Swedish" period I founded a start-up born with the aim of giving users the ownership of their data, thus avoiding profiling by large multinationals. For logistical reasons this experience has been terminated.

Despite having finished my training, I want to keep myself constantly updated and trained by running challenges known as "CTFs" at the HackTheBox portal, in which I have the opportunity to put into practice (in a harmless way) the use of dedicated frameworks for hacking, like Kali Linux, Metasploit, Armitage, nMap, ZAP, Dirbuster, nikTo, AirCrack, IDA, GHIDRA, etc... In my spare time I dedicate myself to reading the latest security newsletters and to seeing the latest POCs videos (or reports) regarding zero-day vulnerabilities.
